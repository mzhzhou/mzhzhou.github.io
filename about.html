Work

I am...

listening

I believe that being a good designer means being an excellent listener. Pain points come up in the depths of conversations and it’s important to make space for reflection.

looking to empower

In my work, I am always trying to explore ways to infuse projects with positivity, care, and purpose, empowering individuals to confidently shape the experiences around them.

curious about ubiquitous things

Many of my inspirations come from noticing quirks in the systems of regular daily life. Digital meets physical, energy grids, transportation, utensils, the list goes on How do tools affect the way humans interact? How do they change assumptions about society?

Talk to me about...

road trips

Being born and raised in New Zealand to road trip-loving parents means that I’ve travelled around the country quite extensively. I’m always nostalgic and eager to get in a car and find places to see, smell, and breathe.

art and form

I was that architecture student you never saw in University, always ready to fix something with a xacto knife, glue, and coffee in hand. Drawing, at the root of the practice, continues to be a core practice for me.

dance and choreography

I studied dance as a minor degree at UC Berkeley, choreographed for a competitive urban dance team, and have danced for 22 years in various genres. Choreography and freestyle movement continues to my foundational inspiration for expression. Please reach out if you want to take class together!
